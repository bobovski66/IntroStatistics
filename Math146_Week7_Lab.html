Math 146 — Week 7 Lab: Two-Sample Inference

Two-sample z-tests and confidence intervals for means and proportions.

Reference Formulas

\[\text{SE}_{\bar{x}_1-\bar{x}_2}=\sqrt{\frac{\sigma_1^2}{n_1}+\frac{\sigma_2^2}{n_2}} \qquad z=\frac{(\bar{x}_1-\bar{x}_2)-0}{\text{SE}} \qquad \text{CI: }(\bar{x}_1-\bar{x}_2)\pm z^*\,\text{SE}\]

\[\hat{p}_{\text{pool}}=\frac{x_1+x_2}{n_1+n_2} \qquad z=\frac{\hat{p}_1-\hat{p}_2}{\sqrt{\hat{p}_{\text{pool}}(1-\hat{p}_{\text{pool}})(1/n_1+1/n_2)}}\]
