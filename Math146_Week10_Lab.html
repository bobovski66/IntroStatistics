Reference Formulas

Regression slope and intercept: \[b = r\cdot\frac{s_y}{s_x} \qquad a = \bar{y} - b\bar{x} \qquad \hat{y} = a + bx\]

Residual: \(e_i = y_i - \hat{y}_i\)  ·  \(r^2\) = proportion of variability in \(y\) explained by the linear relationship with \(x\).

The regression line always passes through \((\bar{x},\,\bar{y})\).
